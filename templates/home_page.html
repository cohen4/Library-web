About Books Are Us

We, at BooksAreUs believe that everybody should be with a book in their hand and a smile on their face.

We know that the world had change, became more digital then ever, but remember- one book is worth a thousand tweets of Kanye West

Our goal is to make people come to our website and feel at home.

So, sit back, relax with a good cup of tea and start searching for the next great book we have to offer!

As Drake once said, we started from the bottom- now were here. Today, we have over 2 branches across the country and over 20 different books- all waiting just for you

And if you don't know where to start, don't hesitate and come to our branches and ask our lovely librarians for help- trust us, they are the best!

We wish you a wonderful experience at our website and branches and happy hunting!

From all the founding partners,

Noa, Yarden and Maayan
